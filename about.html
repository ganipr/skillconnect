About ConferenceConnect

Connecting minds, fostering innovation, and advancing knowledge across disciplines

Our Story

Founded in 2020, ConferenceConnect began as a small initiative to bridge the gap between academic researchers and industry professionals. What started as a local networking platform has grown into a global community of over 50,000 professionals across 120 countries.

Our journey has been marked by a commitment to creating meaningful connections that transcend geographical boundaries and disciplinary silos. We believe that the most groundbreaking ideas emerge at the intersection of different fields, and we've made it our mission to facilitate these cross-pollinations.
