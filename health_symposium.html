Global Health Symposium 2025

Dec 5-7, 2025 Geneva, Switzerland

Addressing contemporary challenges in public health and medical research

About the Symposium

The Global Health Symposium brings together leading health professionals, researchers, and policymakers to address the most pressing health challenges of our time. This year's focus will be on innovative solutions for global health equity, pandemic preparedness, and the integration of technology in healthcare delivery.

Symposium Themes

  • Pandemic Preparedness and Response Systems
  • Digital Health and Telemedicine Innovations
  • Health Equity and Access to Care
  • Climate Change and Health Impacts
  • Mental Health in the Post-Pandemic World
  • Antimicrobial Resistance Strategies

Featured Speakers

We're honored to host these distinguished experts:

  • Dr. Maria Van Kerkhove - WHO Health Emergencies Program
  • Prof. Devi Sridhar - Chair of Global Public Health, University of Edinburgh
  • Dr. John Nkengasong - Director of Africa CDC
  • Dr. Soumya Swaminathan - Former Chief Scientist, WHO

Who Should Attend

This symposium is essential for:

  • Public health professionals and researchers
  • Medical practitioners and clinicians
  • Health policy makers and government officials
  • NGO and global health organization representatives
  • Health technology innovators
  • Medical and public health students
