International Tech Summit 2023

June 15-17, 2023 San Francisco, CA

Exploring the future of artificial intelligence and machine learning applications

About the Conference

The International Tech Summit is a premier gathering of technology leaders, researchers, and innovators from around the world. This year's focus on artificial intelligence and machine learning applications brings together the brightest minds to share groundbreaking research, discuss emerging trends, and explore practical implementations across industries.

Conference Themes

  • AI in Healthcare: Diagnostics and Treatment
  • Machine Learning for Climate Change Solutions
  • Ethical AI and Responsible Innovation
  • Natural Language Processing Advances
  • Computer Vision and Image Recognition
  • AI for Business Process Optimization

Keynote Speakers

We're proud to feature leading experts in the field including:

  • Dr. Samantha Chen - Director of AI Research, TechFuture Institute
  • Prof. Michael Rodriguez - Head of Machine Learning, Stanford University
  • Lisa Thompson - CEO, InnovateAI Solutions
  • Dr. Raj Patel - Lead Scientist, Global AI Ethics Forum

Who Should Attend

This conference is ideal for:

  • AI/ML researchers and academics
  • Technology executives and decision-makers
  • Software engineers and developers
  • Data scientists and analysts
  • Graduate students in computer science and related fields
  • Tech entrepreneurs and startup founders
